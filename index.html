Reform AI PRO

Redesign any room with AI.

Take a photo, pick a style, and see your space reimagined in seconds.

Download on the App Store

How it works

  1. Take a photo or pick one from your library.
  2. Choose the room type and style — Modern, Scandinavian, Industrial, Bohemian and more.
  3. Add optional details (blue sofa, wooden table, remove the lamp…).
  4. Generate and compare before/after with an interactive slider.

Features

  • 12 curated design styles.
  • 8 room types.
  • Drag-to-reveal before/after slider.
  • Automatic archive of your designs.
  • Available in English, Spanish, French, German, Brazilian Portuguese and Japanese.
